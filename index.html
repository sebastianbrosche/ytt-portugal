About Heat Lagos

Heat Lagos sits directly across from Praia de Batata and minutes from Meia Praia. You train where others vacation - 300+ sunny days a year, world-class food, surf breaks nearby.

Both programmes are non-residential. You choose your own accommodation so you can rest, integrate, and enjoy Lagos the way you want. Many students stay in nearby Airbnbs, surf houses, or hotels close to the beach.

Travel, accommodation, and meals are not included. After training, you're steps from the ocean - surf, swim, or just watch the sunset.

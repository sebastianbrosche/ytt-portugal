Your Teachers

Two teachers. Different backgrounds. One shared obsession with what actually works.

Stine

Lead Vinyasa Teacher · 15+ Years Experience

Stine is the heartbeat of Heat Lagos. She has spent over 15 years refining her approach to Vinyasa yoga — not the performative kind, but the kind that teaches you to hold space, read a room, and build sequences that actually serve the people in front of you.

Her teaching is grounded in anatomy, breath, and the practical reality of running classes day after day. She knows what works because she has tested it in hundreds of classes with real students.

Stine leads the 200-hour Vinyasa Yoga Teacher Training alongside Sebastian. She handles sequencing, teaching methodology, anatomy, and the practicum — the part where you actually stand up and teach.

Vinyasa Sequencing Teaching Methodology Anatomy Practicum

Sebastian

Co-Teacher · World BJJ Champion · Movement Specialist

Sebastian came to yoga through Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu — a world where mobility, recovery, and breath control mean the difference between winning and losing. He didn't become a yoga teacher to escape competition. He became one to understand the body better.

His approach is pragmatic, anatomy-driven, and unapologetically focused on what works. He brings an athlete's perspective to the training — understanding how to work with tight hamstrings, stiff shoulders, and the reality of bodies that have been through combat sports.

Sebastian also runs Yoga for BJJ, a programme that brings yoga to martial artists worldwide. His BJJ background means he understands the athlete's body in a way most yoga teachers don't.

What You Will Learn

You'll learn how to create and teach strong, purposeful Vinyasa classes that students genuinely love and want to return to.

Slow Flow

Learn to teach gentle, breath-led classes that build awareness and calm. Perfect for beginners, recovery days, and creating space for students to reconnect with their bodies.

Yang Yin

Dynamic movement followed by deep release. You will learn how to balance effort and surrender in one class, giving students both the workout they want and the stretch they need.

Peak Pose Classes

Theme-based sequencing built around a target posture. Backbends, inversions, arm balances. Learn progressive warm-ups, intelligent preparation, and how to make the peak feel achievable.

Strength Building Flow

Classes designed to build functional strength through movement. Smart progressions, regressions, and how to challenge students without losing the flow or the breath.

Hands-On Adjustments

When, where, and how to touch. Ethics of hands-on work, reading a student's readiness, and giving adjustments that genuinely help rather than distract.

Workshops

How to design, market, and deliver workshops that sell out. From inversions intensives to backbends masterclasses - the business and the teaching behind special events.
